## Key Concepts to Explore

- Multi-channel delivery with user preference resolution
- Priority queues and rate limiting per user/type/provider
- Fan-out patterns for broadcast vs targeted notifications
- Delivery tracking and feedback loops
- Notification aggregation and digest strategies
